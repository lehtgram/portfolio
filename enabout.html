About me

I am a social services student with a particular interest in holistic well-being and building safe, trusting interactions. In my work, I strive for calm encounters, active listening, and practical problem-solving.

I have a prior education in information and communication technology (programming), which has strengthened my ability to utilize digital tools in my work. During my studies, I am focusing especially on mental health and adult social work.

Additionally, I hold a driving license as well as a valid occupational safety card and hygiene pass.

My Areas of Interest

  • Supporting mental health and everyday functioning
  • Society, psychology, science, and history
  • Utilizing digital tools in social work

I actively develop my skills through independent projects and approach continuous learning with purpose and curiosity.

Free Time
